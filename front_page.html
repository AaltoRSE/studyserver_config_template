PolAlpha: Pilot study for Digital Behavior and Social Media Exposure

PolAlpha is an academic research study conducted by Aalto University investigating how digital platforms shape user behavior and content exposure patterns.

What We're Studying

We examine social media consumption, search behavior, and algorithmic content discovery across Google services. This is a pilot study and data collected will not be used in actual research.

Who Can Participate

Anyone living in Finland who has an Android device.

What's Involved

Participants securely share activity data mobile devices via the AWARE framework. Optionally sharing Google activity data and TikTok activity data will aid in the study.

Data & Privacy

All data is stored securely at Aalto University, and used exclusively for this research. You can withdraw and request data deletion at any time. Our study complies with GDPR and Aalto's research ethics policies.
